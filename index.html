About Us

Ave Maria School is found in Namutumba Town Council.

Classroom Environment

We have a conducive classroom enviromnent which is full of learning aids and instructional materials that enables incidental learning among pupils.


We have well trained professional teachers who handle the pupils with care and deliver quality content to them.

The classrooms which the pupils study from are strategically located in a noise-free environment that may disrupt the teaching-learning process.

Our Services

Below are some of the services our school provides:

Academics

We provide education services to learners of all levels ranging from Nursery to Primary Seven.

Religion

Ave Maria is a Catholic founded school with a strong religious foundation. Our school ensures that it educates pupils who are God fearing citizens.

Sports

We also participate in sports activities like soccer, netball, volleyball and we have won several trophies during those sports competitions.

Tours

We conduct educational tours and trips to different parts of the country to enable learners experiment and relate what they learn in class in their day-to-day life.

Entertainment

Since work without play makes Jack a dull boy, we organise days for entertainment to break boredom among learners.

Guidance and Counselling

Our school provides guidance and counselling to the boy and girl children to prevent them from making bad decisions. We have professional senior men and women who handle issues of our pupils confidentially.
